My Resume

My name is

Chantel Campise

. I am a 24 year old US Army veteran and up-and-coming full stack web developer.

I was introduced to web development through an all women's hackathon in my hometown

Buffalo NY

, I actually still have a sticker I got there on my laptop to this day. Getting to see the process of web pages being developed from nothing into these beautiful websites was truly inspiring.

Design is really important to me. Growing up I went to a performing arts school. I really got to see the affect that art has on people, from dissecting other peoples artwork in school to dissecting artwork at art galleries and museums. A person never truly realizes what a piece of art did to them until they read the explanation and it describes exactly what was felt and why. I want to create things that have an affect on the people that view it. I want to be the cause of the effect that makes someones day better. I'm someone that is constantly thinking about how I am affecting the space around me therefore I am always thinking about things from others point of view. I want to understand other people so that I can help them in my own way.
